7 WAYS TO SAVE THE TURTLES:

1. Don’t buy souvenirs or other items made from critically endangered hawksbill shell.

2. Reduce your carbon footprint!

3. Avoid buying seafood or choose responsibly caught seafood.

4. Just say NO to plastics!

5. Join a sea turtle conservation tour!

6. Donate to a worthy cause.

7. Choose sunscreen carefully.
